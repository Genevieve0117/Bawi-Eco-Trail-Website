Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is there a reservation fee for the cottages? ▼

Yes, to secure your cottage, a reservation is required. You can book your spot easily through our official booking form on this website.

2. How to I get to Bawi Eco Trail by public transport? ▼

We're excited to have you! Here are the best ways to commute to our location in Padre Garcia, Batangas:

From Manila (e.g., Cubao, Buendia, PITX):

  1. Take any Batangas-bound bus (like JAM Liner or ALPS) and ask for a ticket to Rosario, Batangas. (Some routes may require a transfer in Lipa City, but direct trips to Rosario are available).
  2. Once you arrive at the Rosario bus terminal, take a jeepney or tricycle to the Padre Garcia town proper (Poblacion).
  3. From the town proper, hire a tricycle and ask the driver to take you directly to Bawi Eco Trail in Barangay Bawi.

From Batangas City (e.g., Batangas Grand Terminal):

  1. Take a bus or jeepney bound for Lipa City.
  2. From Lipa, take another bus or jeepney to the Rosario bus terminal.
  3. Once in Rosario, take a jeepney or tricycle to the Padre Garcia town proper.
  4. From there, hire a tricycle to take you to Bawi Eco Trail.
3. Is the place PWD and senior citizen accessible? ▼

Yes, we have made our main pathways and facilities accessible for senior citizens and persons with disabilities.

4. Is Bawi Eco Trail Pet Friendly? ▼

Yes, we love our furry friends! You may bring your pets as long as they are leashed and you are responsible for cleaning up after them.

5. Is there a strong internet service in Bawi Eco Trail? ▼

Yes, the area has a stable mobile signal, so you can stay connected and share your moments!

6. Is there Parking free? ▼

Yes, we have a secured parking area for our guests. The parking fee is:

  • Cars/Vans: ₱20
  • Motorcycles: ₱10
7. Does Bawi Eco Trail accept card payment? ▼

We accept Cash, GCash, and PayMaya for all on-site payments (entrance fees, cottages, and food stalls). We do not accept credit or debit cards at this time.

8. Do I need to pay an entrance fee if I already booked a cottage? ▼

Yes, the entrance fee is separate from all cottage booking fees. All guests entering the eco-trail are required to pay the standard entrance fee.

9. Is there a Cancellation Policy? ▼

All confirmed bookings are final and non-refundable.

10. Is there an entrance fee for children? ▼

We welcome adventurers of all ages! Children 5 years old and below can enter for free.

11. When is Bawi Eco Trail open? ▼

We are open daily from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

12. Can I make a reservation via phone call? ▼

Yes, you can! For assistance with your booking or to make a reservation over the phone, please call us at +639266561419.

13. How can I confirm the reservations? ▼

If you booked online: After submitting our booking form, you will receive a confirmation email or message with your reservation details and payment instructions.

If you booked by phone: Our staff will confirm your details and provide a reference number during the call.

14. Can I bring my own food and drinks? ▼

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and drinks to enjoy a picnic with your family and friends. We just ask that you help us keep the park clean by practicing "Clean As You Go."

15. Are there dining options in Bawi Eco Trail? ▼

Yes! If you don't want to bring your own food, we have several food stalls on-site offering a variety of snacks and refreshments.

16. Can I stay overnight? ▼

To help preserve the natural beauty of the area, Bawi Eco Trail is currently a day-use facility only. We do not offer overnight accommodations.

17. Is there electricity in the cottages? ▼

Yes! Our cottages are eco-friendly and equipped with solar-powered charging ports for your basic charging needs (like mobile phones and power banks).

18. Is it safe to swim in the river? ▼

Your safety is our priority. Swimming is generally safe and allowed under two conditions:

  1. The weather is good, and the water level is safe.
  2. Our security personnel and lifeguards on-duty have given a "go" signal.

Please always check with our staff before entering the water.

19. Is the natural spring water drinkable in Bawi Eco Trail? ▼

Yes, the water from our natural spring is potable and safe to drink.

20. Does Bawi Eco Trail offer activities besides swimming? ▼

Currently, our main attractions are swimming and relaxing by the river. We encourage guests to enjoy the natural scenery, have a picnic, and unwind in our cottages.
